# Privacy Policy for Tito

**Last Updated: November 16, 2025**

## Introduction

Tito ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email Address**: Collected when you sign in with Google
- **Profile Information**: Name and profile picture from your Google account
- **Shift Data**: Work shifts you create, including dates, times, hourly rates, and notes
- **Goals**: Weekly hour targets you set for yourself

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App interactions and feature usage
- **Authentication Tokens**: Secure tokens to maintain your login session

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Tito app functionality
- Allow you to track your work shifts and hours
- Calculate your earnings and progress toward goals
- Sync your data across devices
- Authenticate your identity through Google Sign-In
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider
- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- **Authentication**: We use Google OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
- **Access Control**: Only you can access your personal shift data when logged in

## Third-Party Services

Tito uses the following third-party services:

### Google Sign-In
- **Purpose**: User authentication
- **Data Shared**: Email address, name, profile picture
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Supabase
- **Purpose**: Database and backend services
- **Data Stored**: User profile, shift data, goals
- **Privacy Policy**: https://supabase.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your shift data with employers or other users
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Share your information except as described in this policy

We may share information only in these cases:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your personal data stored in the app
- **Update**: Modify your shift data and goals at any time
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Download your shift data (via app export features)
- **Revoke Access**: Disconnect Google Sign-In authorization at any time

### How to Delete Your Data
To delete your account and all data:
1. Open Tito app
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, email us at madebyarv@gmail.com to request account deletion.

## Children's Privacy

Tito is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups**: Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting a notice in the app (for material changes)

Continued use of Tito after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

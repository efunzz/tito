Delete Your Tito Account

We're sorry to see you go! You can delete your Tito account and all associated data using either of the methods below.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your shift data, goals, and settings will be permanently deleted.

How to Request Account Deletion

Email Deletion Request

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

Email: madebyarv@gmail.com

Subject: Tito Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • The email address associated with your Tito account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (email, name, profile picture)
  • All shift records and work history
  • Weekly goals and settings
  • All associated data stored in our database

Data Retention

After deletion:

  • Your data is immediately removed from active systems
  • Backup copies are deleted within 90 days
  • You can create a new account at any time, but previous data cannot be recovered
